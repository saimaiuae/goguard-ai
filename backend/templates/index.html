⚽ Player Injury Risk Analysis System

Upload a match video, and our AI will analyze player movement to predict potential injury risks.

Using advanced deep learning and computer vision, we track sprint count, minutes played, and fatigue level.

🔍 How It Works:

  • ✔️ Upload a video containing player movements.
  • ✔️ AI detects players & extracts movement data.
  • ✔️ System analyzes sprint intensity and fatigue.
  • ✔️ Generates an injury risk report for each player.
